\slide{
  What Was Done in January?
  \begin{multicols}{3}
  \fact{zgreen}{Zold SDK}{%
    We created a Software Development Kit (\href{https://github.com/zold-io/zold-ruby-sdk}{SDK}) for Zold online
    wallet management system, \href{https://wts.zold.io}{WTS}. Thanks
    to this SDK, Zold can be seamlessly integrated into a crypto-exchange,
    a mobile wallet, or an online shop, to receive and send payments
    in ZLD. Open source enthusiasts are working already on a Java
    copy of the SDK. More programming languages are coming.}
  \fact{zgreen}{Zold memory leakage fixed}{%
    Over the last few months we've had a very annoying
    technical issue in Zold software, our experimental non-Blockchain
    cryptocurrency. Simply put, our nodes were running out of memory
    every few hours and had to be rebooted. A long investigation
    helped us reveal a bug in Ruby, and we managed to implement
    a workaround, which stabilized all nodes enormously. At the moment
    they run for days without any issues. We still have some technical
    problems in Zold software, they are listed in the
    \href{https://blog.zold.io/roadmap.html}{Roadmap} (was updated recently).}
  \fact{zgreen}{YouTube video blogging}{%
    Yegor Bugayenko, our CEO, started a regular video blogging in
    his YouTube channel, focusing mostly on management questions
    and the process Zerocracy is developing. The feedback is very
    positive up to date, we are getting new sign-ups from programmers,
    new potential investor requests, and our overall media coverage
    is growing, thanks to this initiative. Aside from that Yegor is
    now publishing weekly reports about our business and technical
    progress, subscribe to our \href{https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCxZAzmY_OPw40I1-YStWqhw}{YouTube channel}
    to follow.}
\end{multicols}}
