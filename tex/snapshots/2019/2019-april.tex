\slide{
  What Was Done in April?
  \begin{multicols}{3}
  \fact{zgreen}{Zold was listed on AlterDice}{%
    As we planned, Zold, our non-Blockchain experimental cryptocurrency
    was listed on its first exchange: \href{https://alterdice.com}{AlterDice.com}.
    It is a very important step for us, which demonstrates
    to our users, investors, and programmers, that the technology
    we've been developing for over a year, is recognized by the
    market and is needed. We are planning to get listed on other
    exchanges soon, we are in the negotation process with over a dozen
    of them. By the way, thanks to the growth of BTC, the rate of
    ZLD is \$1.51 now.}
  \fact{zgreen}{Online Risk Manager}{%
    We created a new satelite project for Zerocracy customers,
    to help them manage risks in their projects and perform
    risk-driven development the way it should be done, with qualitative
    and quantitative analysis, planning, and so on. The tool is
    called \href{https://www.0rsk.com}{0rsk.com} and is open source
    and free for all users. It is still in beta-testing.}
  \fact{zgreen}{Full Integration with Bitcoin}{%
    We've implemented our own integration with the Blockchain of
    Bitcoin, which we didn't have before and had to use
    Blockchain.info and Coinbase. This was a pretty complex
    technical task, and we managed to complete it perfectly. Currently,
    our \href{https://wts.zold.io}{WTS} platform maintains our own
    private keys, sends and receive Bitcoin payments. We still use
    Coinbase to purchase BTC when a new funding payment arrives
    for a Zerocracy project.}
\end{multicols}}
